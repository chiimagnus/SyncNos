Highlight
"The fundamental challenge is that people readily generalize from a few experiences, and they are surprisingly resistant to change once they have developed a model of the way things work."
This explains why users often struggle with new interfaces that don't match their mental models. It's important to design with familiar patterns.
Highlight
"Good design is actually a lot harder to notice than poor design, in part because good design fits our needs so well that the design is invisible, serving us without drawing attention to itself."
Note
The concept of affordances is crucial in UI design. A button should look clickable, and a text field should look editable. This book provides great foundational principles for designing intuitive interfaces.
Highlight
"Human-centered design means starting with a good understanding of the people who will use the products or services and the activities for which they will use them."
